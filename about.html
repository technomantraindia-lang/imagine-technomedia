Our Workflow

Structured Creative Flow

A disciplined pipeline designed to manage complexity, align vision, and deliver pristine finishing results.

01

Client Brief

We start by aligning with your objectives, studying reference links, script direction, and timing metrics.

02

Planning & Strategy

Mapping structural data pathways, establishing server storage setups, and setting critical check milestones.

03

Editing (Offline Assembly)

Trimming raw sequences, structuring story logic, and establishing structural pacing and sync points.

04

VFX & Graphics

Generating composites, wire removals, graphics animations, and necessary visual fixes.

05

Color Grading (DI)

Injecting photographic looks, balancing highlight zones, and enhancing dramatic contrast patterns.

06

Sound Design

Balancing ambient zones, cleaning vocal captures, placing sound effects, and final stereo/surround mixing.

07

Review & Quality Control

Scrutinizing technical renders for export glitches, sound drops, and ensuring specs match target parameters.

08

Final Mastering & Delivery

Packaging high-res masters formatted specifically for theater projections, television nets, or OTT grids.
